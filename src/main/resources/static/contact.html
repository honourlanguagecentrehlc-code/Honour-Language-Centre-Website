Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for a course?

You can register for our courses by visiting the "Courses" page and clicking the "Apply Now" button for your desired course. This will redirect you to our online registration form. Complete the form with your details and follow the payment instructions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile banking (KBZ Pay, Wave Money), and online payment gateways. Specific payment instructions will be provided during the registration process.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds within 7 days of purchase if no course content has been accessed. Partial refunds (50%) are available within 14 days of the course start date. Please refer to our Terms of Service for complete details.

Do I need any special equipment for online classes?

You'll need a stable internet connection, a computer or smartphone with a camera and microphone, and the Zoom application installed. We recommend using headphones for better audio quality during live sessions.

Can I access course materials after the course ends?

Yes, enrolled students have access to course materials for 6 months after the course completion date. This allows you to review and practice at your own pace.
